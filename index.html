About G.O.T Vote NYC!

Get Out the Vote NYC (GOT Vote NYC) is new nonprofit created with a mission to increase the number of registered voters in New York City, the number of voters going to the polls. We also strive to educate voters on the job responsibilities of elected positions, the candidates running to fill the positions and any ballot measures.
